About Us

Welcome To
Babylon National School

We are a community of passionate educators dedicated since 1996 to providing a dynamic and engaging learning environment for all our students. Located at Shantinagar, Kathmandu, our experienced teachers work tirelessly to inspire and motivate students to achieve their full potential.

We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive school culture that fosters a love of learning, creativity, and collaboration. We are committed to empowering students to become lifelong learners who are equipped with the 21st-century skills and knowledge they need to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Babylon National School is a co-ed English medium school offering education from Pre-Grade to Secondary level. Our holistic approach to education ensures that every student — academically, emotionally, and socially — is given every opportunity to flourish.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality, holistic education that nurtures every child's intellectual, emotional, social, and physical development. We are committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where students are inspired to become critical thinkers, responsible citizens, and compassionate leaders who contribute positively to society and the world.

Our Vision

To be Kathmandu's premier English medium school recognized for academic excellence, innovative pedagogy, and character development. We envision a school community where every student discovers their unique potential, develops a global perspective, and graduates with the confidence, skills, and values needed to make a meaningful difference in an ever-changing world.

Our Philosophy

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Our educational philosophy is rooted in the belief that every child is a unique learner with unlimited potential. We combine the best of traditional values with modern, research-backed teaching practices to deliver an education that is both rigorous and joyful.
